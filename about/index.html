About

An agency built like a product team.

We started Apex Fractional in 2024 to run the kind of growth function we always wished we had access to — senior operators, AI-native workflows, real accountability, no retainer rot.

Story

Why Apex exists.

Most agencies optimize for the monthly retainer, not the outcome. Most in-house hires can't ship as fast as the tools now make possible. We built Apex to sit in the gap.

We're a small team of ex-founders and growth leaders. We embed with five to ten ambitious companies at a time. We build AI workflows, brand systems, and content engines that you own — then we stay on to run them for as long as you want us to.

If we're doing our job, you see compounding output month over month. If we're not, you cancel — month-to-month, no lock-in. That's the deal.

How we work

The rules we run by.

Ship in your accounts.

Everything lives in your tools. No hostage-taking dashboards, no proprietary platforms you lose when we leave.

Month-to-month, always.

No annual contracts, no claw-backs. You stay because the output is worth it, not because the paperwork says so.

Senior operators only.

No junior hand-offs. The person you meet in the demo is the person running your account.

AI where it earns its keep.

We use AI for leverage, not as a buzzword. If a human does it better, a human does it.

Real outputs, not mockups.

Week two you review actual drafts, campaigns, and sequences — not slide decks about what we'll do.

Transfer on request.

When you're ready to bring it in-house, we hand over every workflow, brand kit, and prompt. On purpose.
