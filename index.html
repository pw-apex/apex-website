Process

How an engagement unfolds.

Month-to-month. Weekly live calls. Everything we build transfers to you.

Day 1–2

Kick-off and access

Goals set, priorities aligned, access provisioned — GSC, GA, CMS, CRM, and ad accounts as relevant.

Day 3–5

Integration and brand kit

We wire the stack together and build your brand kit — voice, tone, product context, competitor context, audience definitions.

Week 1–2

First outputs ship

Drafts, sequences, or campaigns land for your review. Not mockups — actual outputs running in your accounts.

Week 2–4

Calibration

Two to three rounds of feedback to dial in voice, accuracy, and quality.

Week 4+

Production mode

Steady output, weekly live calls, continuous optimization. Monthly steering for larger engagements.

Most clients stay 6+ months
