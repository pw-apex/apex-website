Background

The client.

Luxury Presence builds websites and marketing technology for the top agents and brokerages in real estate. They had a full content and demand team, strong brand, and a high-intent audience — but the top of the funnel was leaking.

Generic landing pages converted fine but didn't give prospects a reason to engage before a sales call. The team needed something that showed value immediately.

Challenge

Why they called us.

The content team was writing two long-form pieces a week and still felt like they were falling behind. Paid was working but expensive. Sales wanted better-qualified leads coming into the pipe.

They didn't need another agency running the same playbook. They needed something that demonstrated the product's intelligence before the first demo.

What we built

The scanner.

Apex designed and shipped a custom AI-powered website scanner that prospects could run against their own agency site in 30 seconds. The scanner surfaces opportunities specific to real estate — SEO, AEO, accessibility, performance — in a report branded for Luxury Presence.

We built the tool, wired it into their CRM, wrote the prompts and analysis logic, and set up automated follow-up. It now sits as the primary call-to-action on their homepage.

In parallel, Apex took over the content engine — cutting the content team's hands-on writing time by 60% while raising publishing cadence.

Results

What happened.

Within 60 days the scanner had been run thousands of times and became the #1 top-of-funnel source of qualified demos. Leads coming through it converted at 3× the rate of other channels because prospects had already seen the intelligence of the product.

The content team got their nights and weekends back. Luxury Presence expanded the engagement into a second phase covering paid and outbound.
