Privacy Policy

Last updated April 16, 2026.

Apex Fractional ("we", "us") operates this website and provides AI-implementation and marketing services to clients. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

Information you give us

If you book a demo, email us, or fill in a form, we collect your name, email address, company, role, and anything else you choose to share in that conversation.

Information we collect automatically

Standard server logs (IP, user agent, referrer, timestamps) and basic analytics events (pages viewed, device type). We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking pixels on this site.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry and schedule a call.
  • To operate and improve the site.
  • To send occasional follow-ups about your inquiry. We do not run cold email or marketing sequences against form submissions.

Third parties we work with

We use a small number of service providers, each under their own privacy terms:

  • Website hosting and CDN.
  • A scheduling tool for the booking page.
  • A transactional email provider for replies.
  • A privacy-conscious analytics tool.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie to make the site work and (optionally) a privacy-first analytics cookie. You can block either in your browser without affecting functionality.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to show you what we have, correct it, or delete it. Email privacy@apexfractional.com and we'll respond within seven days.

Data retention

Inquiry and client records are kept for as long as we have an active relationship, plus up to two years for legitimate follow-up. Server logs are rotated out within 30 days.

Children

The site is not directed at anyone under 16 and we don't knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If we update this policy we'll change the "last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be announced on the home page for 30 days.

Contact

Questions about this policy? privacy@apexfractional.com.
